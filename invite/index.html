The Heart of Rocking Life

FORGE Groups: Where Transformation Happens

Everything in Rocking Life centers on one thing: a small group of men who meet every week to grow deeper in faith, build real friendships, and let the Holy Spirit do the work. We call them FORGE groups. They follow a biblical discipleship model: life-on-life, in person, over time.

Weekly. Every Week.

FORGE groups meet every single week because consistency builds the trust that makes transformation possible.

Small. Up to 12 Men.

Big enough for different perspectives. Small enough that you can't hide. Every man in the room is known.

Deep Friendships. Real Purpose.

Men come looking for direction, connection, and something that actually matters. FORGE groups deliver all three — and the friendships you build here last a lifetime.

The Holy Spirit Leads.

The group leader creates the space. The men show up honestly. But the transformation? That's God's work. We create the conditions and trust Him with the outcome.

What This Asks of You

An hour and a half every week. Honesty about where you really are. Willingness to read Scripture daily. The courage to let other men see the real you. And over time, the willingness to do this for someone else.

This doesn't cost you money. Rocking Life is a nonprofit ministry and membership is free. But it will cost you your comfort, your masks, and your excuses. That's the real price of becoming who God created you to be.

What Changes

Discipled. Transformed. Equipped to Disciple Others.

The goal isn't to attend a group. The goal is to be so transformed by the Holy Spirit that you begin doing for other men what someone just did for you.

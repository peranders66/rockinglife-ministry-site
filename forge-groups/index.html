FORGE Groups: Where Transformation Happens

Everything we do centers on one thing: a small group of men who meet every week to grow deeper in faith, build real friendships, and let the Holy Spirit do the work.

What Makes FORGE Different

We call them FORGE groups. They follow a biblical discipleship model: life-on-life, in person, over time.

Weekly. Every Week.

FORGE groups meet every single week because consistency builds the trust that makes transformation possible.

Small. Up to 12 Men.

Big enough for different perspectives. Small enough that you can't hide. Every man in the room is known.

Deep Friendships. Real Purpose.

Men come looking for direction, connection, and something that actually matters. FORGE groups deliver all three — and the friendships you build here last a lifetime.

The Holy Spirit Leads.

The group leader creates the space. The men show up honestly. But the transformation? That's God's work. We create the conditions and trust Him with the outcome.

The Discipleship Journey

From Invited to Inviting Others

1

Personal Invitation

Every man enters because someone in the community personally invited him.

2

Join a FORGE Group

Weekly meetings grounded in Scripture, prayer, and honest conversation. This is where the real work happens, and where the Holy Spirit meets you.

3

Be Transformed

Over months and years, God works through the relationships in your group to shape you into the man He created you to be.

4

Disciple Others

When you're ready, you invite another man in and eventually lead a group of your own. Every disciple becomes a disciple-maker.

"Go therefore and make disciples of all the nations [help the people to learn of Me, believe in Me, and obey My words], baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe everything that I have commanded you; and lo, I am with you always [remaining with you perpetually — regardless of circumstance, and on every occasion], even to the end of the age."

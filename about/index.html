About Rocking Life

A Christ-centered community where men are transformed by the Holy Spirit, equipped to disciple others, and committed to walking alongside each other.

Who We Are

We ignite and inspire the hearts of men. One friend, one conversation, and one transformed life at a time.

Our Vision

To inspire and equip men around the world to experience God's love through deep friendships, transform their lives, and impact their families and communities by living with purpose.

Our Mission

We train men to see through facades, walk alongside other men through proven discipleship pathways, and become the kind of men who do the same for others.

What We Believe

The Holy Spirit transforms men. We create the conditions — honest relationships, consistent community, and Christ-centered accountability — and trust God to do what only He can do.

How We're Different

Rocking Life is invite-only. Every man who joins is personally invited by someone who already walks with us. No mass marketing. No open enrollment. That's by design.

The Story Behind Rocking Life

It Started with a Fire and Seven Men Who Showed Up

In 1999 in Seattle, our founder Per Angerstig was trying to start a fire with a freshly cut tree. It wouldn't light. So he used dry logs and twigs to get the fire going, then gradually added the fresh branches. Eventually, a massive fire was burning and hundreds of embers rose into the night sky.

"I felt God say: this is how the Kingdom will spread all over the world. Embers touching down everywhere, igniting new fires."
Per Angerstig — Founder, Rocking Life

Years later in 2015, Per went through a devastating divorce that brought loneliness, depression, and suicidal thoughts. Seven men saw through his mask and showed up. They didn't try to fix him. They called. They invited him for coffee. They sat with him. They stayed.

That experience became the foundation for Rocking Life. A movement built on a simple truth: when men have the courage to show up for each other, the Holy Spirit does the rest.

Rocking Life launched in 2024 from Spring, Texas and now serves men across the United States, Australia, and Europe.

An Invite-Only Community

We Grow Through Relationships, Not Campaigns

Rocking Life is not open enrollment. You can't sign up on a website or join through a link. Every man who enters our community is personally invited by someone who already walks with us and sees something in him worth investing in.

It's a biblical model of discipleship: personal, relational, and with purpose.

Know someone in Rocking Life? Ask them about becoming a member. That's how it starts.
